Who I Am


My name is Thomas, and I have always been interested in infrastructure of all kinds! You might say that growing up in New York City, I have witnessed first-hand how crucial infrastructure is keeping any village, town, or city running. I love that, as a civil engineer, I can impact the built environment all around me and make a positive difference in the lives of hundreds, thousands, and even millions of people. While I find all facets of civil engineering fascinating, my main areas of interests currently lie in structural engineering and transportation engineering. Most of my coursework has been directly relevant to structural engineering, and I recently interned as a transportation engineering intern to find out more about the industry.

I am currently an undergraduate senior studying towards a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering at Cornell University. I cannot wait to start making an impact in the world upon graduation, no matter where in civil engineering I may end up!
